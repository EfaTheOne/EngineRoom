Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an incorporated company?

No. We accept founders at the idea stage. Incorporation support is part of the program.

Do you take equity?

Zero equity. We charge a flat annual fee of $1,000 to cover operational costs.

Is there a physical requirement?

Yes. This is a hybrid program centered around The Station in Cochrane, AB.

What is the selection process?

Applications are reviewed weekly. Shortlisted candidates are invited for a pitch interview.
